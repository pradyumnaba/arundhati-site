About us

इदं गुरुकुलं नाम ब्रह्मशक्ति प्रबोधकम्। सद्विचार सुचारित्र्य स्वान्तस्सत्वप्रकाशकम्||

Arundhati gurukulam is a bold and innovative step taken in our Bramhin society under the Divine leadership and directions of HH Shri Satyatma teertha swamiji of Uttaradi matha with objective of providing cost effective, knowledge oriented education to children in safe environment. It is one roof under which modern education of global standards, samskruta legacy and cultural heritage is blended and imparted such that the child is enriched to build a better society.
